Hi, I'm Marilyn. I am currently attending the Denver University Coding Boot Camp and look forward to using the skills I learn throughout this course to become a successful developer. I remember getting my first computer in elementary school, I was the only one on my block to have one. It was a Macintosh, and all I remember is playing Super Munchers and Dr. Brain on it. After being a part of the coding boot camp, I can truly appreciate how significant of an advancement these programs were then, and even more how the possibilities are truly limitless!

When I am not studying or working on homework, you can find me at a coffee shop or a table-top game store. I also like venturing around Denver in search of the perfect food spot.Care to join me in a delicious lunch and game of Ticket to Ride? I love driving with my windows down, music turned up loud, on the highway late at night, but at the same time I enjoy cozy nights in curled up with the latest crime fiction book or on my computer playing World of Warcraft. I believe that I am capable of achieving my goal to become a web developer. I believe in hard work, being a team player, continual learning and the willingness to go above and beyond.
